# Anduril's Product Lines

**Q:** What are Anduril's product lines?

**A:**

Anduril Industries spans software, hardware, and autonomous systems. The unifying idea: **Lattice is the brain; everything else is a sensor or effector that plugs into it.**

## Core Software Platform
- **Lattice OS** — flagship AI-powered command-and-control software. Open platform that fuses sensor data, autonomously detects/tracks/classifies threats, and coordinates assets. Nearly all hardware runs on it.
  - **Lattice for Mission Autonomy** — teaming/autonomy SDK.

## Air / Counter-UAS
- **Anvil** — autonomous quadcopter interceptor that rams hostile drones; **Anvil-M** adds a munition.
- **Roadrunner / Roadrunner-M** — reusable, recoverable VTOL twin-jet interceptor (-M is explosive counter-air variant).
- **Pulsar** — modular electronic-warfare system for counter-drone/EW.

## Uncrewed Aircraft (drones)
- **Ghost** — autonomous VTOL reconnaissance helicopter (sUAS).
- **Altius** — tube-launched loitering munitions / UAS family (Area-I acquisition).
- **Fury (YFQ-44A)** — autonomous fighter-class jet; entry for USAF **Collaborative Combat Aircraft (CCA)** program.
- **Bolt / Bolt-M** — man-portable VTOL loitering munition (Bolt-M is the lethal variant).

## Maritime / Undersea
- **Dive-LD / Dive-XL** — autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs) for long-range subsea (Dive Technologies acquisition).
- **Ghost Shark** — extra-large AUV developed with the Royal Australian Navy.
- **Copperhead** — autonomous underwater vehicle / torpedo family.

## Ground / Surveillance
- **Sentry Towers** — autonomous surveillance towers (radar, EO/IR on Lattice); US border + base security. Variants: Sentry, Maritime Sentry, mobile.
- **Wisp** — passive thermal/EO sensor tower.
- **Menace** — deployable, ruggedized expeditionary C4 (command, control, communications, computing) platform.

## Space / Other
- Space and ISR efforts.
- **Solid rocket motor** manufacturing (Adranos acquisition) — propulsion for missiles/munitions.
- **IVAS** — Army's HoloLens-based soldier headset program, which Anduril took over from Microsoft.
