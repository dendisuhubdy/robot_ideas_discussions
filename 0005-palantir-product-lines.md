# Palantir's Product Lines

**Q:** What are Palantir's product lines?

**A:** Palantir has four main product lines:

## 1. Gotham
The original platform, built for **government, defense, and intelligence** customers. Integrates and analyzes disparate data sources (signals intelligence, reports, sensor data) to support operational decision-making — target identification, mission planning, counterterrorism, battlefield awareness. Historically what Palantir was known for.

## 2. Foundry
The **commercial/enterprise** counterpart to Gotham. A full data-integration and operations platform that creates a digital "twin"/ontology of an organization — modeling its data, logic, and decision-making in one place. Used across manufacturing, healthcare, finance, energy, and logistics for supply-chain optimization, operational analytics, and workflow automation.

## 3. Apollo
The **continuous delivery / deployment infrastructure** layer. Autonomously manages and updates Gotham, Foundry, and AIP across diverse environments — cloud, on-premise, classified networks, edge devices, even disconnected environments. Enables Palantir to ship SaaS into environments traditional SaaS can't reach.

## 4. AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)
The **newest** line (launched 2023). Connects LLMs and other AI to an organization's private data and operations within Foundry/Gotham, with governance and guardrails. Enables LLM-powered agents and workflows that can take real actions against enterprise systems, not just chat.

---

**Quick framing:** Gotham (government) and Foundry (commercial) are the two core platforms; Apollo is the deployment backbone underneath; AIP is the AI layer on top.
